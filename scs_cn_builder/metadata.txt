It integrates land use/land cover raster data, Hydrologic Soil Group raster data and basin polygons to generate CN2 maps for average antecedent moisture conditions, CN3 maps for high antecedent moisture conditions, basin-level zonal statistics, quality-control summaries and an HTML calculation report.
